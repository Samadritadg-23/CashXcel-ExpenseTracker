How to use CashXcel

  • Set a spending limit to track expenses (balance stays unchanged).

  • When adding income (salary, pocket money, etc.), choose Save only.This increases your balance without affecting your spending limit.

  • If someone owes you money, click the pen icon in the sidebar and note it down!

  • Track money you set aside in the savings corner!
